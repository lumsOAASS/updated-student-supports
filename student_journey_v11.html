About this initiative — Over the past 1–2 years, LUMS has developed a structured approach to identifying and supporting first-year students early in the semester. This map traces a student's journey from Orientation through to year-end, showing how the university responds to different levels of concern — from open resources available to all, to intensive coordinated support for those who need it most. Every pathway is designed around one principle: no student should struggle alone.
